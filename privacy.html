Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

Introduction

MA PLAST GROUP ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website, browse our products, or contact us through any channel.

By using our website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the site.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to serve you:

  • Cart contents: Product selections and quantities you add to your shopping cart during your browsing session.
  • Contact details: Name, phone number, email, or message content when you reach out via WhatsApp, phone, or email.
  • Technical data: Browser type, device information, and IP address collected automatically for security and performance.

We do not collect payment information, credit card details, or banking data through this website. All payments are handled directly between you and our team via phone or WhatsApp.

Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses browser localStorage to temporarily remember your cart items while you browse. This data is stored entirely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels. If we add analytics in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill product orders and inquiries.
  • To communicate with you regarding your requests, deliveries, or support questions.
  • To improve our website experience and product offerings.
  • To maintain security and prevent fraudulent activity.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with trusted third-party services to enhance functionality:

  • WhatsApp: Used for order placement and customer communication. Messages sent via WhatsApp are subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps: Embedded maps to help you locate our store. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Font Awesome & Google Fonts: Used for icons and typography. These services may receive your IP address when loading assets.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. We recommend avoiding the transmission of sensitive personal data through unsecured channels.

Cart data stored in your browser's localStorage is device-specific and is cleared if you clear your browser data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Clear your browser cache/localStorage to remove cart data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page indicates when the current version took effect. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
